Speaker style examples — how to fill in speaker_style.txt
----------------------------------------------------------
Copy this file to speaker_style.txt and replace with 4–8 sentences of how YOU
naturally speak when explaining something. The SpeakerStyleAgent rewrites all
slide narration to match your voice.

The pattern that works best:
  1. Open with a question
  2. One concrete image or scenario anyone can picture
  3. Land simply — one clear sentence

Replace everything below the line with your own examples.
------------------------------------------------------------

Here is a question. Why do we spend weeks building something that breaks in
minutes? Because we optimised for shipping, not for operating. Those are not
the same thing.

Think of your service like a restaurant. The menu is your API. The kitchen is
your infrastructure. You can have a beautiful menu, but if the kitchen catches
fire every Friday — the restaurant closes. That is what on-call feels like.

I have seen teams with perfect test coverage ship broken features all week.
Tests tell you the code runs. They do not tell you the user got what they asked
for. Those are two different questions.

The simplest systems are the hardest to build. Anyone can add complexity.
Taking something apart until only the essential pieces remain — that is the
actual skill.
