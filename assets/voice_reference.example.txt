Voice reference transcript — example format
------------------------------------------
Copy this file to voice_reference.txt and replace with the EXACT words you speak
in your voice_reference.wav recording. F5-TTS uses this to align voice cloning.

The transcript must match the audio word-for-word — including filler words.
Record 14–30 seconds in a quiet room, front-facing, consistent pace.

Example:
  "Hi, I'm [your name]. In this session we'll cover [topic].
   We'll explore [concept 1], [concept 2], and [concept 3].
   By the end, you'll have a clear picture of how these work together."

Tips:
  - Speak at your normal presentation pace, not slowly
  - One continuous sentence or two short ones works well
  - Avoid background noise, fan hum, or reverb
  - Trim to the first word onset: ffmpeg -y -ss <start_offset> -t <duration> \
      -i your_recording.wav -ac 1 -ar 24000 -sample_fmt s16 assets/voice_reference.wav
